About Me

Greetings~ My name is Jiacheng Zhang. I am first-year PhD student advised by Professor Steve Oney. Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from University of Michigan. I also obtained another degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interest lies in Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence.
During my undergraduate years, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Xinyu Wang and Prof. Tianyi Zhang in web automation systems. I was also advised by Prof. Andrew Owens in multimodal learning and computer vision.

  Touch and Go: Learning from Human-Collected Vision and Touch
Fengyu Yang*, Chenyang Ma*, Jiacheng Zhang, Jing Zhu, Wenzhen Yuan, Andrew Owens NeurIPS (Datasets and Benchmarks Track), 2022                    

A dataset of paired vision-and-touch data collected by humans. We apply it to: 1) restyling an image to match a tactile input, 2) self-supervised representation learning, 3) multimodal video prediction.
